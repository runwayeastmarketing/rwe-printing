Recommended setup

Follow these steps to get set up quickly. Once done, printing is as simple as sending a file and releasing it at the printer.

1

Create your account

Sign up on the Uniflow portal. You'll receive a unique PIN code - this never changes, so make a note of it. You'll use it at every printer.

Sign up →
2

Install the print driver & download the app

Install the driver to print directly from your computer, and download the app to upload documents and release jobs from your phone.

Print driver

Installs a virtual printer on your computer. Print any document as you normally would and it goes straight to your queue.

Download driver →

Mobile app

Upload documents and scan the QR code at the printer to release your jobs - no PIN entry needed.

3

Send your document to the queue

Choose whichever method suits you - your job will be held in your personal queue until you release it at any Runway East printer.

Print driver

Open your document and print as normal - select Secure Printing Input as your printer.

Mobile app

Open the app, tap Upload, and select your file.

Web portal

Log in at runwayeast.uk.uniflowonline.com and upload your document directly.

4

Go to any printer & release your job

Walk up to any printer across any Runway East site and release your job using one of the two methods below.

Scan QR code with the app

Open the Uniflow app and tap Scan QR Code, then point your camera at the QR code on the printer. In the app, go to My Queue, select your documents and tap Release.

The QR code only works with the Uniflow app - your phone's camera won't work.

Enter your PIN code

Type your PIN code on the printer's touchscreen to sign in and release your jobs. Your PIN is shown in the portal and never changes.

Other ways to print & scan

Email your document as an attachment to our print address. Your job will appear in the queue, ready to release at any printer with your PIN.
